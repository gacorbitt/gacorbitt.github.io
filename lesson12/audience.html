Target Audience

The site can be used by couples with young children and older retired church members that are looking to take trips to locations with temples nearby. They are looking for a hotel that supports everything they need for a temple trip including daycare, temple history and long term stays.



Tom and Jessica are 33 and 32 years old respectively. He is a dentist and she is an elementary school teacher. They have two children ages 5 and 8. They are interested in learning more about church history and exposing their children to different areas of the United states. They plan on taking one temple trip a year.

Wendell and Karen are a retired couple that want to visit various temples in both the United States and worldwide. They are interested in visiting all of the temples in the areas described in their family histories and completing ordinance work there. They are looking for extended stays at each location and moving directly from one temple site to another.

Scenarios

  • 1. How long will we be staying?
  • 2. What else is there to do in the area?
  • 3. Is there more than one temple we can visit before leaving?
  • 4. Are there many families with children that stay here?
  • 5. Do we have any family in the area we can visit?
