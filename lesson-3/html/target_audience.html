Target Audience

The site can be used by local college students and young families looking for something to do on the weekend and people from anywhere else looking to spend some time seeing what the area has to offer and willing to pay a little to do that. Ages can range from 18 to 80+ and the average annual income could be from $20,000 and up.

Tom and Jessica

Tom and Jessica are 24 and 22 years old respectively. They are both full time students at BYU-I in Rexburg, ID, and when they aren't in class they're always looking for something nearby they can do without spending too much money. Day trips and weekends are ideal for their schedule and budget. After graduating they plan on raising a family in the area so right now they're focused are finding spots good for children.

Robert

Robert is a 55 year old small business owner from Arizona. He is self employed so he can pursue his passion for outdoor sports. He comes to Idaho every year because is has the best bear hunting outside of Alaska. He knows that being from Arizona he isn't used to the cold so he has to know exactly what to expect when he goes to Idaho.


Scenarios

  • 1. Is is getting warmer or colder during the week?
  • 2. Is it going to snow enough to go skiing?
  • 3. Can I dress my kids warmly enough or do we need to find somwething else to do?
  • 4. Are we sleeping in a tent or a motel?
  • 5. Does going to Bear Lake mean a sweater or a swim suit - or both?
