Design Principles Document

Greg Corbitt

Contrast

Oregon Ducks

goducks.com

The Oregon Ducks site uses differing shades of gray and black in the background and then use bright green and yellow colors with white text to breakup the page and highlight what it is they want you to see.

White Space

Apple

apple.com

Apple is a master at using white space to showcase their products and keep their website clean and professional. This is an extension of what they promote their products to be - simple and elegant.

Alignment

Union Pacific

up.com

Union Pacific's alignment is top notch. They keep everything consistent at each screen size and they even change shapes from curves and circles on a larger screen to rectangular boxes on a small screen to keep the alignment looking good.
