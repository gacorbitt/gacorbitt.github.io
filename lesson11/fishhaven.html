Fishing and more

Fish Haven was originally named Rush Creek, but it didn't take long for the name Fish Haven to stick when the early settlers realized they were living next to a lake that had some of the best fishing they had ever seen. That lake is called Bear Lake and it still boasts some of the best fishing around. In fact this is the only lake on this side of the world that has Bonneville Cisco

Times have changed though, and people come here for more than just the fishing. From the north shore you can walk out into the lake for a few hundred yards and barely get your knees wet. This is a prime spot for families with kids and you'll see your fill of jet skis racing around the area. We have cabins and boat rentals for your stay, but you better book early because in the summer things fill up fast.

The winters are tough here but that doesn't keep people away either. We offer ice fishing for trout and cisco and our bear and deer hunting is listed as some of the best in the western United States.
