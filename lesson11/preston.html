We're more than Napoleon Dynamite

Welcome to the home of Napoleon Dynamite - the sleeper hit movie of 2004. We still celebrate that success with a festival in the summer, recreating the Tetherball Tournament, Tater Tot eating contest, Moon Boot dance and Football throwing contest from the movie, but that's not all we have to offer.

In July we have the Franklin County Parade and the Famous Preston Night rodeo. There's nothing better than taking in the sites during the day and then coming out to relax with great food and even better entertainment as you watch the barrel racing and calf roping, and of course it wouldn't be a rodeo without bull riding.

We look forward to seeing you here in our lovely little town of 6,000 people. There's something to do for everyone and it's not hard finding the space to enjoy whatever you want to do to relax. Pack plenty of clothes though, because once you come to Preston you may just not want to leave.
