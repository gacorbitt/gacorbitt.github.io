Biggest Little City in Idaho

At just over 3,000 people, when you visit Soda Springs you'll feel right at home with everyone you meet. That's why we call ourselves the biggest little city in Idaho. Everyone treats everyone like family here, and we wouldn't want it any other way.

Soda Springs is nestled down at the base of the Rocky Mountains in Caribou Couty. From the moment you enter the town you'll realize where wew get our name. When the town was established back in the early 1900's there were hundreds of carbonated springs located all over the area. This was a frequent stop for indian tribes and a landmark for the Oregon Trail.

We even have our own geyser that goes off every hour and is as powerful today as the day it was capped. Come and see your Idaho family, you'll be glad you did.
